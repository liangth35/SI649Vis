Gun violence is a severe issue in the US.

The number of gun laws in each state increases steadily each year.

More gun laws have a possible correlation with several outliers.

The increase in number of gun laws doesnâ€™t necessarily mean a better suppression on gun violence.

With an arc plot, we can interpret the findings more intuitively. Each arc line represents a target instance: a state with a year. The green and blue part show the gun violence death rates and injury rates without the extra gun laws.

From the plots, it can be observed that there are many instances that have more violence when there are new laws.

Suicide and Homicide are the two major component of gun related death.

Comparing the other gun laws, the laws dedicated against suicide is limited.
